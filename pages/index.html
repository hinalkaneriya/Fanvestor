Our
Story

In a visit with a friend in 2017, FanVestor’s founder, Michael Golomb got his first glimpse of fan investing. A prominently displayed a photo of the Green Bay Packers on the wall started a conversation, and Michael learned of a program that the Green Bay Packers had launched to give fans shares in the team. He recognized an immediate overlap in fandom and investing. 

As FanVestor was conceived, the modern celebrity and fan relationship was changing, the creator economy was exploding, and regulations were evolving to allow for new and innovative means to crowdfund businesses.

Offerings

We have a full suite of regulatory approvals for both equity crowdfunding and digital securities suites that power Web2 and Web3 initiatives.

  • S-NFTs & INVESTMENTS

    We support Regulation Crowdfunding offerings, Regulation D and A+ offerings, and all can be built for Web3 or Web2, with fractional S-NFTs or without. 

  • PRODUCTS

    Our new product launches and product offerings provide an extensive array of e-commerce and tiered purchase options to fans and can include perks and benefits.

  • CHARITY

    Our Charity fundraising engine is robust and can accommodate any charity or charitable initiative that creators aim to amplify.

  • NFTS

    Creator-driven NFTs purchased on our site are minted on the Ethereum, Polygon, or Stellar blockchains and can be resold at any of the popular NFT marketplaces.
